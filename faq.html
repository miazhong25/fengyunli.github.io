Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is your MOQ?

MOQ is 50 pieces per design for T-shirts, 30 for hoodies. Lower quantities may be possible for samples.

2. Can I get a sample?

Yes! We offer paid samples. Shipping cost is borne by the buyer. Sample time: 3–5 working days.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (Bank Transfer), PayPal, and Western Union.

4. How long is production time?

7–15 days after sample approval and deposit received.

5. Do you support customization?

Yes! We offer full OEM/ODM service — logo, packaging, size, color, material.
