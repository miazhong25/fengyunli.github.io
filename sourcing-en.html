Sourcing Services

We assist international buyers in sourcing high-quality products directly from verified factories in China. Whether you're an Amazon seller, e-commerce brand, or retail business, we act as your trusted local partner on the ground.

Our Sourcing Process

· Requirement Analysis: Understand your product specs, target price, and quality standards.

· Supplier Matching: Identify and shortlist reliable factories based on your criteria.

· Factory Audit: Conduct on-site visits to verify production capacity and compliance.

· Sample Coordination: Arrange sample production and shipping for your approval.

· Production Monitoring: Track manufacturing progress and perform pre-shipment inspections.

· Logistics Integration: Coordinate with our warehousing team for labeling, consolidation, and shipping.

By leveraging our deep local network and industry expertise, we ensure you get the best value, quality, and service—without the language barrier or time zone challenges.
