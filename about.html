About Us

We are a factory located in Dongguan City, manufacturing clothes hangers, home furnishings, and handicrafts. Our products are sold on e-commerce platforms in China and also shipped to countries such as the United States, Japan, Russia, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Australia, and Fiji via sea, air, and land transportation. In addition, we offer low-cost warehousing services. Overseas customers can send products from different suppliers to our warehouse, where we will repackage them and ship them to their respective countries at discounted rates. We support photo and video verification to assist with product inspection..
