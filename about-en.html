About Us

We are a professional manufacturer based in Dongguan, China, specializing in eco-friendly clothes hangers and home furnishings. Our products are exported to Europe, North America, and Asia.

We also offer comprehensive logistics services for overseas clients, including goods receipt, warehousing, repackaging, labeling, and consolidated international shipping.

You may choose to have your own courier pick up the shipment, or use our shipping solutions—including sea freight, air freight, and land transport—to deliver your goods worldwide.
