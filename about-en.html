About Us

We are a professional hanger manufacturer based in Qiaotou Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, China. Our product range includes plastic, flocked, dipped-coating, aluminum, and rubber hangers, as well as home cleaning and storage solutions.

Our products are distributed both domestically—through wholesale, retail, and major e-commerce platforms—and internationally, with key markets in Japan, the United States, Southeast Asia, Russia, and Australia.

Leveraging Dongguan’s status as a global manufacturing hub and international logistics center, we offer overseas clients a comprehensive one-stop supply chain service, including:

·Local sourcing assistance

·Warehousing

·Repackaging and labeling (including Amazon FBA-compliant labels）

·Consolidated international shipping via sea, air, or land freight

By combining multiple shipments into a single consolidated delivery, you can significantly reduce your international shipping costs. Should you have any specific requirements, please feel free to contact our customer service team—we are committed to supporting overseas buyers throughout the entire process, from procurement to final delivery, ensuring a safe, smooth, and worry-free experience when receiving goods from China.
