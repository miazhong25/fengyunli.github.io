About FENGYUNLI STORE

We are a factory located in Dongguan City, Guangdong Province, China, specializing in the production of:

  • Clothes hangers (plastic, wood, metal)
  • Home furnishings and decorative handicrafts
  • Custom OEM/ODM products for global brands

Our products are sold on major Chinese e-commerce platforms such as Taobao, Tmall, and Pinduoduo, and exported worldwide via sea, air, and land transport.

We also provide low-cost warehousing, repackaging, and quality inspection services for overseas customers. You can request photo or video verification before shipment.

Wholesale, bulk orders, and custom designs are welcome!
