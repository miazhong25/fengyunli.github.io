Consolidation & Warehousing Services

Many buyers already work with multiple Chinese suppliers, but each shipment is often too small—below 100 kg for air freight or under 1 m³ for sea freight—to qualify for competitive shipping rates. As a result, international logistics costs become disproportionately high, significantly increasing the landed cost per item.

Since Chinese products are already highly price-competitive, international freight often determines your final profitability. That’s where our consolidation service adds real value.
