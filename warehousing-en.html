Warehousing Services

Our warehouse is strategically located in Qiaotou Town, Dongguan City—just 1 hour from Shenzhen Port and 1.5 hours from Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport. This prime location enables fast and cost-effective export logistics.

What We Offer

· Secure Storage: Climate-controlled space for your inventory.

· Inventory Management: Real-time tracking of stock levels.

· FBA Labeling: Compliant barcode printing and application.

· Repackaging & Kitting: Custom packaging per your requirements.

· Order Consolidation: Combine goods from multiple suppliers into one shipment.

· Global Shipping: Sea, air, or land freight to your destination.

By consolidating shipments in our warehouse, you can reduce international freight costs by up to 40% compared to direct factory-to-door shipping. We handle everything so you don’t have to.
