Warehousing & Consolidation Services

Consolidate Your Shipments, Save on Freight

If your suppliers ship small quantities separately, each shipment incurs high international freight costs. Instead, send all goods to our warehouse in Guangdong. We consolidate them into one shipment, reducing your shipping cost by up to 50%.

Our Workflow

1. Receive Goods

Suppliers ship directly to our warehouse

2. Storage

Safe storage until your requested date

3. Repacking

Repack, label, or customize as needed

4. Shipping

Consolidated shipment via your preferred forwarder

Service Fees

Storage: $0.50 / cubic meter / month
Consolidation: $10 / shipment (min. 1 CBM)
Labeling: $0.10 / item

We also offer Amazon FBA labeling and barcode printing.
