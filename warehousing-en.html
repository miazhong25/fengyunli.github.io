Consolidation & Warehousing Services

Many buyers already work with multiple Chinese suppliers, but each shipment is often too small—below 100 kg for air freight or under 1 m³ for sea freight—to qualify for competitive shipping rates. As a result, international logistics costs become disproportionately high, significantly increasing the landed cost per item.

Since Chinese products are already highly price-competitive, international freight often determines your final profitability. That’s where our consolidation service adds real value.

Why Choose Our Warehouse?

Strategic Location
Our facility is in Qiaotou Town, Dongguan, Guangdong—just a short distance from Shenzhen Port, Shenzhen Bao’an Airport, and Guangzhou Baiyun Airport. This makes it an ideal hub for cargo consolidation.

Dedicated Storage Space
We offer secure, segregated storage areas exclusively for international clients, equipped with 24/7 surveillance to prevent loss or damage.

Seamless Coordination
Simply share our warehouse address with all your suppliers. Once they ship goods to us (with tracking numbers), we’ll inspect arrivals, take photos/videos for your confirmation, and safely store your items until you’re ready to consolidate.

Value-Added Services

We also provide:

  • Inventory counting & quality inspection
  • Repackaging, relabeling, and carton replacement
  • Volume optimization to reduce shipping costs

Transparent & Flexible Shipping

When your consolidated shipment is ready, we’ll provide a clear packing list (total cartons, dimensions, gross weight). You can either:

  • Use our trusted air/sea freight partners—we’ll offer competitive quotes, or
  • Arrange pickup directly with your own forwarder (no handling fees).

All charges are clearly itemized—no hidden costs. Our goal is simple: affordable, fast, and worry-free service to build long-term partnerships.
